bio

I graduated from the Australian National University with a Bachelor of Advanced Science in 2013. Since 2014, I have been a PhD candidate at the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics of the Australian National University.

During my undergraduate and graduate studies, I primarily focused on data analysis and interpretation. This includes visualising data, writing formal reports and publishing peer-reviewed articles in scientific journals. I also worked on a sky survey delivering a large data set to the international scientific community. In this project, I was responsible for data testing and producing final data products.

I am proficient at object-oriented scripting (especially Python), working with Unix servers, data analytics and data visualisation.
